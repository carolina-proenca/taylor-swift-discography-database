6.Which songs are collaborations or feature other artists? Provide the names of the songs and the respective featured artists, ordered alphabetically by the names of the featured artists.

Query:

SELECT DISTINCT s.song_title, p.person_name
FROM Discography d
NATURAL JOIN People p
NATURAL JOIN Songs s
WHERE d.role_name='Artist' AND p.person_name!='Taylor Swift'
ORDER BY p.person_name;
