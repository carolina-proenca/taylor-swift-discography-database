9.Which songs were released after 2010?

Query:

WITH tab AS(SELECT s.song_title
FROM Discography d
NATURAL JOIN Songs s
WHERE s.song_release_date NOT LIKE '200%'
GROUP BY s.song_title)
SELECT COUNT(*) num FROM tab;
