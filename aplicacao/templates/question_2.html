2.Which songs have ‘Taylor's Version’ in their name and are tagged as “Ballad”? Order them alphabetically by the song names.

Query:

SELECT s.song_title
FROM Discography d
NATURAL JOIN Songs s
NATURAL JOIN Tags t
WHERE s.song_title LIKE "%Taylor's Version%" and t.tag_name='Ballad'
GROUP BY s.song_title
ORDER BY s.song_title;
