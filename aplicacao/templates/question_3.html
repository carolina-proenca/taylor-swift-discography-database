3.What are the songs in the category ‘Non-Album Songs’? Provide the name, URL, and release date, ordered by the release date.

Query:

SELECT DISTINCT s.song_title, s.song_url, s.song_release_date
FROM Discography d
NATURAL JOIN Songs s
WHERE s.category='Non-Album Songs'
ORDER BY s.song_release_date;
